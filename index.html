Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

⌨️

Keyboard-native

⌘L to search, ⌘T for a new tab, ⌘1–9 to jump between tabs. Every action has a shortcut so your hands never leave the keyboard.

🛡

Built-in ad blocking

Block ads and third-party trackers at the network level. See exactly how many were stopped — right on your new tab page.

🗂

Vertical sidebar

A collapsible tab sidebar that pins to a floating icon bar when you want it out of the way. Drag tabs to reorder. Your workflow, your layout.

🔒

Private windows

Spin up a private session in one keystroke. No history, no cookies, no trace — closed the moment you're done.
